Since 1981, Audi Sport has stood for “Vorsprung durch Technik” in international racing. The engagement in 2023 consists of marathon off-road rallying and customer racing. The Dakar Rally with the Audi RS Q e-tron¹ and its innovative drivetrain² is the flagship factory motorsport programme. Audi Sport customer racing sees models from Audi competing all over the world in the hands of customer teams. The road-going models from the brand with the four rings profit from the expertise that comes from racing.
